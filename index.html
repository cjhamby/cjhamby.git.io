Some Things About Me

I’m a recent graduate from NC State University, now seeking the next step in my career. My B.S. in Mechatronics Engineering covered a broad range of Electrical and Mechanical engineering concepts, leading some people to nickname it the “Jack of All Trades” degree. One of the best aspects of my degree is it’s relevance to an abundance of applications. It equipped me to adapt to the various challenges associated with electromechanical systems.

I especially enjoy working on electrical engineering projects. The fields which interest me most are:

  • RF
  • Embedded Systems
  • Analog Circuits
  • PCB Design

Building this site has been a side-project for me to learn about web development. I’m working on adding a blog page where I talk about my learning experiences and share info that would’ve saved me some time.

Thank you for visiting!
